I. C.A.N.

I CAN is a nonprofit that teaches young kids how to stay safe in bullying and peer pressure situations. I CAN stands for Independance, Assertiveness, Confidence, and the Necessity to Say No. We teach through fun activities and real-world scenerios that could happen to anyone. I CAN helps build character traits as well such as Confidence and Perseverence
